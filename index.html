Why choose snapsite?

Snapsite is a platform to automate the archive process of photos on building sites. It can be Easily integrated in your digital eco-system.

camera

Shoot

Take photos trough our native mobile application, available for iOS and Android.

map

Locate

The app will locate the nearest building site.

folder

Archive

Snapsite allows you to archive photos with the structure you like, you can have sub-folders in building sites and manage permissions on them for your team mebers.

people

Collaborate

Snapsite is built to be used with your team, you can manage permissions on building sites and on folders. You can also invite guest to collaborate on specific building sites or folders.

construction

Integrate

Snapsite is ready to be integrated with your digital eco-system, trough Zapier the most popular integration software or directly with Snapsite APIs.

lock

Secure by default

Snapsite is built on newest cloud technologies, to be secure and reliable.
